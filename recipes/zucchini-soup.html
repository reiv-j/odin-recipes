Zucchini soup

Creamy soup full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • Zucchini
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Carrots
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the onion and the garlic together for a few minutes
  2. Add the carrot and then the chopped zucchini
  3. Cook for a few minutes and cover everything with water
  4. When everything is soft, mix everything together
