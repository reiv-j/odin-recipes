Sweet Chili Tofu

Stir fried spicy tofu with a touch of sweetness.

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Chili flakes
  • Soy sauce
  • Sugar
  • Corn starch

Steps

  1. Cut the tofu in small bites and coat them with corn starch
  2. Stir-fry the pieces of tofu until golden brown
  3. Mix the rest of ingredients with some water and pour it with the tofu
  4. Wait until the sauce is absorbed by the tofu and enjoy!
