Vegan Meatballs

These delicious vegan meatballs are made with textured soybeans. Paired with a good sauce, they will be your new favorite.

Ingredients

  • Textured soybeans
  • Oats
  • Powdered garlic
  • Powdered onion
  • Salt
  • Bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Place the textured soybeans in water and wait for them to soften
  2. Chop everything up until you have a soft mix.
  3. Form your meatballs!
